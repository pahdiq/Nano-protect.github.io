Our company uses a specialized particle called a "Copper Nano-particle" to protect you and your family.
The way this product works is extremely simple in design, but wil save lives.
"Copper-Nanoparticles" are very small pieces of copper. to give you an idea on the general size,
if you took something the size of your pinky nail, and cut it into a million slices, you would have
one million nano-particles! These particles kill any germs they come in contact with!
Using a simple mechanized sprayer, we can apply these particles intermixed with water.
Once the water evaporates, the particles are left behind, leaving a barrier of protection!
